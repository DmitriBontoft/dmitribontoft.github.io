Ballon de servance

This route winds up the side of a valley folllowing the path of a waterfall 1and river before continuing along a ridge to the peak.

There's a refuge nesr the Ballon where it is possible to stay for the night. There's a trough with a robinet. The water here is indicated as non-potable but I drank it without ill-effect.
