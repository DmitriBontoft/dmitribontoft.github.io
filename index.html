Mountain Bike Trails

  • Ballon

A pet project

Mountain bike trails are often difficult to find without having been to an area before. Here's some I've discovered.

I've included maps and photos to help you get an idea of the trails

Ballon de Servance

The ballon de servance is a bare mountain top in Alsace, not far from the famous Planche des Belles Filles. The surrounding valleys are densely wooded. The trees provide good protection from the sun during the summer.

Moosch

The town of Moosch nestles in a valley in the Vosges. There are multiple trails that lead up both sides amongst trees. Typically for the region these ultimately lead to high pastures
